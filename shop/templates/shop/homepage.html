About us

At Glisty Skincare, we believe that nature holds the key to truly healthy skin. Our mission is to provide gentle, effective, and natural skincare solutions for all skin types—whether you're dry, oily, sensitive, or a beautiful blend of them all.

Every product is crafted with care, using plant-based ingredients, botanical extracts, and skin-loving minerals that work in harmony with your body. We avoid harsh chemicals and choose only the best of what nature and science have to offer.

Our promise? Clean, cruelty-free, and consciously made skincare that supports your skin’s natural glow—because every skin type deserves to feel nourished, balanced, and radiant.
